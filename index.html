Blackwater Park is a scenic public park located in Navan, Co. Meath, Ireland. Nestled along the banks of the River Blackwater, the park offers a tranquil setting for visitors of all ages to enjoy nature, outdoor activities, and relaxation.




Navan parkrun

What is Navan parkrun?

A free, fun, and friendly weekly 5k community event. Walk, jog, run, volunteer or spectate – it's up to you!

When is it?

Every Saturday at 9:30am.

Where is it?

The event takes place at Blackwater Park, Windtown Road, Navan, Co. Meath, C15 KX2D (co-ordinates are 53.663055, 6.693795).

What does it cost to join in?

Nothing - it's free! but please register before you first come along. Only ever register with parkrun once and don't forget to bring a scannable copy of your barcode (request a reminder). If you forget it, you won't get a time.

How fast do I have to be?

We all take part for our own enjoyment. Please come along and join in whatever your pace!

Rainbow bench in Blackwater Park

Beautifully vibrant rainbow bench in Blackwater Park is a hearteningly symbolic gesture of solidarity with the LGBTQ+ community in the town and county, and it's a wonderful addition to the park... that EVERYONE can enjoy!
