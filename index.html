--- title: Blackwater Park Navan description: Blackwater Park is a scenic public park located in Navan, Co. Meath, Ireland. Nestled along the banks of the River Blackwater, the park offers a tranquil setting for visitors of all ages to enjoy nature, outdoor activities, and relaxation. layout: default ---

Entrance features 13 trees planted by the children from Navan national schools.

Blackwater Park Navan is open to public 24/7 all year around.

Blackwater Park Navan is 68 acre park officially opened in 2013.
